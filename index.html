About

I am a postdoc with Damien Calaque at the Institut Montpelliérain Alexander Grothendieck in the ERC Project DerSympApp. I work on the categorification of Gromov–Witten theory via derived geometry and operad theory.

Until this year I was a PhD student supervised by Étienne Mann at the LAREMA and by Cristina Manolache at the University of Sheffield.

I am also an associated member of the ANR project CatAG.

Research interests

  • Derived algebraic geometry
  • Gromov–Witten theory and mirror symmetry
  • Category theory
  • ∞-Operads and bar duality
