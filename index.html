About

I am a postdoc with David Rydh at the Department of Mathematics at KTH.

Before this, I was a postdoc with Damien Calaque at the Institut Montpelliérain Alexander Grothendieck in the ERC Project DerSympApp, and even before that I was a PhD student supervised by Étienne Mann at the LAREMA and by Cristina Manolache at the University of Sheffield, in the ANR project CatAG.

Research interests

  • Derived algebraic geometry
  • Gromov–Witten theory and mirror symmetry
  • Category theory
  • ∞-Operads and bar duality
